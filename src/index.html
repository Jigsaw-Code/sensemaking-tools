A New Way to Understand Conversation at Scale using Google AI

We envision a world where conversations are as impactful as they are engaging. That's why we are developing Sensemaker: an evolving toolkit, leveraging Google AI, designed to help make sense of large-scale conversations.

Sensemaker equips participants, facilitators, and leaders alike to delve into even the largest conversations and to uncover clear, actionable insights.

Why Use Sensemaker?

Jigsaw’s tools for sensemaking preserve the richness of conversation while making it easier to identify patterns, surface areas of agreement and disagreement, and, ultimately, make informed decisions. From analyzing public input to understanding deliberation, Sensemaker offers a smarter way to find clarity in complexity, quickly.

We will continue adding to our library as we develop features for our pilot programs.

How it Works

See What Was Discussed

Identify key topics and themes from the conversation, providing a clear overview of what mattered most to participants.

Grasp Points of Agreement & Disagreement

Highlight where conversation participants agree and disagree, helping users understand different perspectives at a glance.

Get a Conversation Digest

Break down each topic into an easy-to-read summary, making it simple to explore subtopics, key insights, and emerging themes.

Pilot Program

What Could BG Be?

Enabling a Large-Scale Conversation in Kentucky

Bowling Green, Kentucky stands on the cusp of dramatic change. In 2025, Jigsaw partnered with a local innovation agency and the Computational Democracy Project to transform a challenge into opportunity. Read on to see how Sensemaker is already having an impact or explore the final report made possible by our tools.

The Opportunity

Over the next 25 years, Warren County – home to Bowling Green – is projected to nearly double in size, due to multiple factors including the rapid expansion of Nashville just an hour south. Warren County government founded BG2050, an invitation to the residents of Warren County: to not just witness change, but to lead it.

The Conversation

As part of BG2050, Innovation Engine, a local strategy consultancy, launched What Could BG Be? – a month-long online conversation embracing Warren County and surrounding areas. Nearly 8,000 residents participated, submitting ideas for the future that community members weighed in on over one million times, making What Could BG Be? one of the largest online civic conversations ever to be held in the U.S.

Read the blog

The Outcome

Using Sensemaker, local leaders and community members quickly gained a clearer, data-driven understanding of the conversation – uncovering actionable insights in real-time to guide Bowling Green’s growth. The use of AI saved local leaders hundreds of hours sifting through public input.
